A Seamless Workflow for a Better City

Our process is simple, transparent, and designed for effective action.

1. Submit Complaint

Encounter an issue? Easily submit a detailed complaint with photos and location data through our user-friendly portal.

2. Track & Get Notified

Stay informed with real-time status updates. Both you and the public can track the complaint's progress towards resolution.

3. See It Resolved

Once the issue is fixed, the status is updated to "Solved". Your feedback helps create lasting positive change.

The Urban Challenge: Unresolved Issues and Distrust

Even in our most ambitious "smart cities," a fundamental disconnect persists. Crucial issues like broken roads, poor waste management, and faulty streetlights often go unaddressed, leading to frustration and a perception of indifference.

Our portal is designed to fix this, bridging the gap between citizens and city administration through radical transparency.
